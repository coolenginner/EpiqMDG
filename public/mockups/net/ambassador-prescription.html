Get quality prescriptions at competitive prices.

Finding the best price for your prescription medication can be a full-time job. Let Epiq MD help you take the guesswork out of whether you are getting the best price for your medication.

Competitive pricing on name brand and generic prescriptions

Our prescription program ensures that you will get the available prices. The Epiq MD prescription programs can save you money at the neighborhood pharmacy of your choice on both brand name and generic prescriptions

Two programs to choose

Epiq MD offers customers two unique discount prescription programs: An everyday discount program, which is included in our Premier Service Plans and a Wholesale Prescription Program for customers dealing with chronic illnesses or those who have to take prescription drugs for a long period of time. Epiq MD doctors will call in your prescriptions when medically necessary. No need to switch your pharmacy.
