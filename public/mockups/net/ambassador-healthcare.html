The State of Healthcare

Approximately 76 million Americans, from all walks of life, various ethnicities and a variety of income levels are considered underinsured or uninsured. Why is this? It is primarily because of the high cost of traditional health insurance coverage for both individuals and small to medium size businesses that tend to hire most Americans today. The average annual cost for major medical insurance coverage for a family of four is $18,000; according to analyst, this is a problem that negatively impacts the working class, middle class and the upper middle class – - It impacts Americans from all walks of life. Ironically, 44% of workers are employed in low-wage jobs with a median annual wage level of $18,000.

To make matters even worse, 70% of workers work for Employers who don’t even offer insurance benefits. This is why we believe that Telemedicine and Telewellness is a catalyst for massive positive change; by coupling our primary care services with preventative and wellness programs, we believe that it can even improve the quality of life for millions of people. Thanks to technology, telemedicine has become a much easier alternative than seeking traditional healthcare. According to the American Medical Association, 70% of doctor’s visits and 40% of emergency room visits are unnecessary. Many of these conditions can be handled effectively with a telemedicine consultation; moreover, many chronic illnesses and diseases can be mitigated through early detection or early warning signs.

The Epiq MD Vision is to improve the quality of life for the 76 Million Americans who do not have insurance or are underinsured. Our slogan is “You deserve an Epiq life.”, and our services are designed to provide alternative healthcare solutions and options to this demographic that is falling through the cracks of a broken healthcare system.
