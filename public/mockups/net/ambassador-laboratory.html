Lab Diagnostics

Prevention begins with information.

Approximately 50% of the average medical chart is comprised of laboratory data; furthermore, diagnostic test results (including blood tests), drive approximately 70% of medical decisions.

Why does Epiq MD provide Laboratory Services?

According to the National Institutes of Health (NIH), the uninsured are less likely to receive high quality or efficient care, this means that chronic conditions are less likely to be managed effectively. Moreover, the likelihood of early detection and prevention are also dismal for the uninsured and underinsured. In order to have an Epiq life, having access and knowledge is critical – this is why we provide Laboratory testing services within our entire suite of products and services.

Lab Services at Affordable Pricing

As a Premium Services Plan member, Epiq MD provides a Complete Blood Count (CBS) blood test for every 12-months of loyal membership. Once your test results are complete, these can be uploaded into your Customer Profile and shared with any of our MD’s via our Telemedicine Platform for discussion and diagnosis, as applicable; all of this at no extra cost. Epiq MD also provides other lab testing services (blood, urine or genetics) on an ala-carte, fee per basis.

Never settle on quality

U.S. based laboratories perform all lab work in accordance withing all industry guidelines and best practices. Moreover, all laboratories part of the Epiq MD network are certified by the Food & Drug Administration (FDA), Center for Medicaid Services (CMS) and the Centers for Disease Control (CDC).

HIPAA-Compliant

Your lab work and results chain of command is performed under the same strict HIPAA –Compliant guidelines that you have come to expect.
