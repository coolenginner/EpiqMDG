Mental Health & Behavioral Therapy

Managing mental health made easy

Our mental and behavioral health is just as important as our physical health. It isn't always as easy to put our finger on our mental health issues, but they are just as real as any other pain that we may experience.

Therapy on your schedule

You can schedule therapy sessions on your time. Choose early mornings, evenings or weekends.

Look beyond geography

It is possible to choose therapists that are not in your geographic region. If you are at a deficit of quality therapists in your area, Epiq MD can connect you with a licensed Master-level therapist from virtually anywhere in the county.

Network of Master Level Therapists

Epiq MD's Master-Level therapists' network offers the same level of professional and discrete therapy as you would receive from live in-person therapy sessions.

Don't sacrifice the quality of care

The AMA has deemed that Telewellness services received via phone or web conference offer the same quality of service as live in-person therapy sessions.

Get the healthcare you need, when you need it.

According to the AMA, almost 70% of all doctor’s visits and 40% of all ER visits have the potential to be adequately handled without loss of quality via phone or video chat with a Telemedicine service like Epiq MD. When you or a family member need to see a doctor, remember we are here to help. Schedule a visit with an Epiq MD doctor and get “cured” from the extra cost and stress of visiting a doctor at the office.
