Frequently Asked Questions

Account

Is Epiq MD a traditional health insurance coverage plan?

Epiq MD is NOT a health insurance coverage plan. Epiq MD provides access to medical doctors and mental health professionals via a technological platform; we provide this service at an affordable fixed monthly price.

Is Telemedicine the same quality of care I would receive at a regular doctor’s office visit?

In many cases, Epiq MD Telemedicine services can offer the same quality care you would receive from an in-person doctor’s visit without travel time, wait time, and a much more cost-effective price point. However, there may be times when the need for medical attention beyond the scope of Telemedicine services and you will have to arrange for an in-person visit with a local doctor. Click Here for a list of the various sicknesses that could be treated via Telemedicine.

What is HIPAA and Is Epiq MD HIPAA-Compliant?

The Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) sets the standard for sensitive patient data protection. Companies that deal with protected health information (PHI) must have a physical, network, and process security measures in place and follow them to ensure HIPAA Compliance. There is no requirement that Epiq MD as a corporation be HIPAA-Compliant; however, the doctors' network and the existing technology platform used to provide primary services are HIPAA-Compliant.

Billing

Is there a contract or long-term commitment required with Epiq MD?

Epiq MD provides both options; 12-month term commitments, which are usually tied to a Nutraceutical or Diagnostic Plan and do encompass an early termination fee. Epiq MD also provides flexible Telemedicine services that you can terminate anytime upon forty-five-days (45) written notice. To cancel your service any time, please send us notice via traditional mail or by email at cancelmyepiqlife@epiqmd.com. Please state your full name, contact information, Epiq MD Customer ID Number and state your desire to cancel. You can expect to be charged a final amount on the next cycle. It's that simple.

Are all of the features described here included with Epiq MD Service Plans?

No, Epiq MD provides a “Basic Service Plan” priced at $21.95/monthly that is limited to only basic Telemedicine service for an individual. Alternatively, Epiq MD also provides its flagship service plans, described as “Premier Service Plans,” which come in individual, couple and family options. The Premier Service Plans include all of the features and benefits described herein; Please Click Here for a Services Price List. There are also specialty products available on an ala-carte basis; these require a fee or price and are available for purchase by anyone.

Services

How to I access Epiq MD Telemedicine services?

Anyone can enroll to be a member of Epiq MD, either through any one of our Brand Ambassadors or at our website; http://epiqmd.com. Once enrolled as a member, you will receive a welcome phone call from our onboarding team and a detailed welcome email. We will walk you through the process of gaining access to our network of doctors and mental health professionals via our Customer Portal. You can schedule appointments with our member service team via telephone, through our member portal or via our app.

Is there a limit to how many times I may use Epiq MD Telehealth services?

Epiq MD does not place a set limit on the number of times you can access services. We encourage all members to seek our services whenever needed. However, we do ask that our members not abuse the service to keep overall costs down for all members.

Can I still use Epiq MD, even if I already have health insurance?

Absolutely! Epiq MD is healthcare agnostic, meaning that our services are available to you if you are uninsured, underinsured or happy with your current health insurance coverage but simply wish to add the Epiq health component to your life. Epiq MD does not report any information to any healthcare companies or government agencies.

How it Works

Are mental and behavioral services included with Epiq MD Telemedicine services?

Yes! What makes Epiq MD unique from many other Telemedicine services in the marketplace is the access to mental and behavioral professionals, diagnostic and laboratory services, life coaching, dietary and nutritional planning and medical services included in all of our Premier Service Plans at no additional cost.

Can Epiq MD doctors prescribe medication?

Yes, when appropriate, Epiq MD doctors can write prescriptions and even call them into your preferred neighborhood pharmacy at no additional cost. Epiq MD members have access to two fantastic prescription options. Our Discount Prescription Program is available to all Epiq MD members at no extra cost and can potentially save you up to 80% on your prescribed medication. Our Wholesale Prescription Program is a much more robust prescription program designed to help you save on the medicines you regularly take. This service is best suited for those dealing with chronic illnesses that require the purchasing of medication for long periods. This program is an ala-carte program and requires an additional fee. Epiq MD doctors do not write prescriptions for any medications listed as Schedule II by the U.S. Drug Enforcement Agency.

Can I enroll my employees on Epiq MD?

Epiq MD is a great alternative or complementary service to traditional health insurance coverage for businesses of all sizes. If you wish to provide your uninsured or underinsured employees with Telehealth services, then Epiq MD would be a good fit for your business. Even if you currently offer health insurance but want to add Telehealth services, Epiq MD may be the answer. Please contact our commercial services team, and they will be happy to conduct an assessment of your business needs at no cost or obligation.
