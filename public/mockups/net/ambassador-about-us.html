About Epiq MD

“We estimate an approximate 76 Million Americans, from all walks of life, various ethnicities and a variety of income levels to be underinsured or uninsured. For most of these families, just having access to a medical professional would be a significant quality of life improvement.” –––Alejandro Rodriguez, Epiq MD Founder

Epiq MD was founded to address the deficit in quality healthcare options for these Americans. We believe that regardless of socio-economic status, You Deserve an Epiq Life. Epiq MD is here to give you what you deserve.

Our Beliefs

To Create Change within our families and friends and to to Create Change within an inequitable healthcare system,

Inspire our communities to pursue an Epiq life, Participate in our communities and Pursue a Return on Involvement

Vision & Purpose: Improving the quality of life for the working and middle class
Mission: We will realize our vision and pursue our Beliefs by providing the following services:

  1. Affordable Healthcare Solutions
  2. Preventive Services
  3. Wellness Products

By joining Epiq MD, you will gain access to our robust national network of doctors and mental healthcare professionals 24 hours a day, seven days a week, 365 days a year. We even offer a 100% bi-lingual experience in Spanish. In addition to our traditional Primary Care Telemedicine platform, our Premier Service Plans include the following benefits:

  • Mental & Behavioral Health, otherwise referred to as Telewellness Discount – access to top mental health professionals available via phone, video chat, or our mobile app.
  • Life Coaching– A life coach is professionally trained to help you maximize your full potential, develop an actionable plan to achieve your goals and reach your desired results.
  • Prescription Service that helps mitigate the high cost of prescription drugs. Our prescription drug services are available at your preferred neighborhood pharmacy, and our network of doctors are more than happy to call in your prescriptions when medically required at no additional cost to you.
  • Laboratory & Diagnostic Services– an Epiq Life means preventative care, which requires early detection. For every 12-months of loyal service, we provide you with a free wellness panel at any one of our in-network laboratories.
  •  Medical Advisory & Planning – services to help you in your greatest time of need. In cases of extreme medical events, which bring on burdensome expenses and debt – Epiq MD is there to help you through the process, including negotiation of these expenses.

These services are available to you monthly with no contract for the price of less than a dollar a month per member. In addition to our monthly services plans, Epiq MD also offers a wide array of ala-carte services. These services include laboratory diagnostics, a wholesale prescription program and a host of science-backed, tailor-made Nutraceutical Products, all intended to help our customers live an Epiq life.

“Up to 70% of doctor office visits and 40% of emergency room visits are unnecessary. Many of these conditions can be handled effectively with a Telemedicine consultation.” - The American Medical Association
